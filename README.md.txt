## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Superstore sales data. The objective is to uncover insights related to sales performance, profitability, regional trends, and customer segments.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- DAX (Calculated Measures)
- Data Modeling

---

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales: 2.3M
- Total Profit: 286K
- Gross Margin: 12.47%
- Average Sales: 230
- Average Profit: 29
- Total Quantity Sold: 38K

## Business Insights
- Consumer segment contributes the highest revenue share.
- Technology category drives maximum sales.
- Certain sub-categories show negative profit margins.
- Regional profitability varies significantly across states.
- High discount levels impact profit margins.
